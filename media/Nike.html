Get Clothing That Flashes

Metal is unyielding and strong. Nike metallic clothing is the same, which makes it perfect for proving your strength during an athletic competition or strutting your stuff when you're out on the town. There are plenty of items in the collection for both adults and kids, like shorts, leggings, hoodies, shirts, and even dresses and swimsuits. They're the perfect addition to your wardrobe.

Shimmer and Shine the Nike Way

There are many reasons why this collection shines. Want close-fitting comfort? The Pro Warm Starry Night Metallic Tights have Dri-FIT tech to keep you dry, and the Leg-A-See Glam Dunk Leggings are built out of stretchy knit fabrics that keep you cool and comfortable no matter how you're moving. The 9” Challenger Brief Shorts are breathable and include pockets for essential storage. The Veneer Training Shorts include a special 3-D structure that holds air between the shorts and your body for immediate cooling. They also have motion vents — you'll never feel constricted in these shorts.

Moving on up, there are shimmering, shining styles for tops. The Metallic Boxed Air Hoodie has a warm hood and a front kangaroo pocket to keep hands warm or to carry keys. It's made of soft, plush Nike cotton — you'll love tossing this item on even if you aren't stepping into the cold. The Metallic T-shirt is a bold design with a standard fit anybody can wear. Show off memorable Nike style and make a good impression anywhere you go. If you're looking for athletic support, the Glam Dunk Indy Bra gives you the comfort you need. Removable pads offer a versatile fit, and the bottom band securely stays put. The Dri-FIT technology means you'll focus on your performance, not on sweat.

Want more? Get the City Series U-Back One Piece Swimsuit for an engineered fit that includes comfortable leg openings, enhanced range of motion, and removable cups. The NSW Air Fleece Dress keeps you cozy and comfortable with a long look. Constructed of sueded fleece, this dress has a ribbed crew neck for a flat fit, and a large kangaroo pocket for versatile storage.

Metallic Clothing Means Business

Build yourself a brand-new outfit from the Nike metallic clothing collection. From athletic gear to everyday wear, you're sure to find a new favorite you'll wear to look and feel like a million bucks.  Finish off your shiny new look with a pair of Nike shoes, and get on the move.
